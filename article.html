Interview with Elza Shirokova

Co-founder and Managing Director at Roasters Specialty Coffee House

What do you find most heartwarming in your guests’ approach?

What matters most to me is the community of loyal guests who choose us every single day. In such a competitive market, capturing someone’s attention is already a challenge—but earning their trust and welcoming them back again is the true achievement.
I am inspired by the stories of tourists who return to Dubai and make Roasters their very first stop, enjoying a cup of coffee and taking beans home as a warm reminder of their journey.
It is especially meaningful when our guests choose to celebrate their most important milestones with us—birthdays, anniversaries, and gatherings of all kinds.
I am grateful for the moments when guests capture memories with our team through photos and videos, many of them now part of the extended Roasters family.
And perhaps most rewarding of all is knowing that a cup of Roasters coffee has become a daily ritual as part of life.
