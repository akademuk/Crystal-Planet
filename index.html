About Us

Founded in 2021 in Dubai, Roasters Specialty Coffee House was born from a deep passion for exceptional coffee and a vision to bring the world’s finest brews to the heart of the UAE.

We believe great coffee is not just about taste—it’s about connection, craftsmanship, and culture. That’s why we work hand-in-hand with dedicated farmers, world-class roasters, and skilled baristas to deliver a consistently exceptional cup, every time.

Today, Roasters stands as a respected name in the specialty coffee landscape, known for its unwavering commitment to quality and innovation. We’ve cultivated a vibrant community of coffee enthusiasts— from curious newcomers to seasoned connoisseurs—who trust us to deliver not just great coffee, but a refined and memorable experience. At Roasters, every visit is an invitation to discover, indulge, and elevate your daily ritual.

Welcome to ROASTERS. Where coffee is more than a drink—it’s an experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to deliver the best coffee and uphold the highest standards in everything we do. From bean to cup, every detail matters. We strive for excellence not only in taste, but in service, design, and experience — creating moments our guests remember and return for.

Proud
of quality

At Roasters, quality begins at the source. We partner with dedicated farmers and trusted producers who share our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and ethical practices. Every bean we select is carefully chosen from renowned farms around the world, based on its origin, harvest conditions, and unique flavor profile.

From the first green bean to the final pour, we honor the craft behind every cup — ensuring that what reaches your table is nothing short of exceptional. Because great coffee starts with great choices.

Sustainability

At Roasters, sustainability is more than a value — it’s a responsibility. We are committed to reducing our environmental impact through conscious choices at every level of our operation.

We believe great coffee shouldn’t come at the planet’s expense.
That’s why we’re building a future where quality, sustainability, and community go hand in hand — one cup at a time.

From sourcing to service, we prioritize eco-friendly practices:

We’ve eliminated single-use plastic across all our branches, choosing compostable and recyclable materials instead.

We work with coffee producers who follow sustainable and ethical farming methods.

We continuously optimize our supply chain to reduce waste, lower emissions, and support long-term environmental health.
